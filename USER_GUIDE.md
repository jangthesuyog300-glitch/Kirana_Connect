# 📦 Kirana Connect — User Guide

Welcome to the ultimate guide for **Kirana Connect**, the hyperlocal marketplace that brings local stores to your doorstep. This guide explains how the three core roles interact with the system.

---

## 🛍️ 1. The Customer Experience
*Effortless shopping at your fingertips.*

The Customer App is designed for speed and convenience. Users can discover the best local stores, browse fresh inventory, and track their orders in real-time.

![Customer Home View](file:///C:/Users/VICTUS/.gemini/antigravity/brain/17932138-cff3-4e51-bbcd-90f26a156917/customer_app_home_1778066758446.png)

### Key Features:
- **📍 Smart Discovery**: Browse stores within a 5km radius.
- **🛒 Dynamic Cart**: Build your grocery list from a single store to ensure seamless delivery.
- **⚡ Live Tracking**: Watch your order move from 'Accepted' to 'Delivered' with real-time notifications.
- **🛡️ Secure Pickup**: Use the unique 6-digit OTP to verify your order at the store.

---

## 👔 2. The Admin Panel (Store Owner)
*Managing your digital storefront.*

Store Owners have full control over their business operations through the Admin Panel. From managing inventory to monitoring staff performance, everything is centralized.

![Admin Dashboard](file:///C:/Users/VICTUS/.gemini/antigravity/brain/17932138-cff3-4e51-bbcd-90f26a156917/store_admin_dashboard_1778066890467.png)

### Key Responsibilities:
- **📊 Business Analytics**: View daily sales, top-selling items, and commission reports.
- **👥 Staff Management**: Add and remove workers, and monitor who is currently busy with an order.
- **📦 Inventory Control**: Quickly add products from the **Master Catalog** or create custom local items.
- **💰 Financial Transparency**: Automatically calculated commission rates and net earnings for every order.

---

## 🛠️ 3. The Worker Interface
*Precision in fulfillment.*

Workers are the engine of Kirana Connect. Their interface is optimized for high-efficiency order processing in a fast-paced environment.

![Worker Order View](file:///C:/Users/VICTUS/.gemini/antigravity/brain/17932138-cff3-4e51-bbcd-90f26a156917/worker_order_view_1778066906247.png)

### The Workflow:
1. **📥 Assignment**: Workers see a list of 'Placed' orders and can accept them to start processing.
2. **⚖️ Preparation**: Once accepted, the worker moves the status to 'Preparing' and gathers the items.
3. **✅ Ready for Pickup**: After packing, the worker marks the order as 'Ready', notifying the customer instantly.
4. **🔄 Optimization**: Workers are locked to one order at a time to ensure 100% accuracy and speed.
